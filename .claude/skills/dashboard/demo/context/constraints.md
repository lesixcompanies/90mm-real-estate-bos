# Constraints

## System Constraint (Persistent)

**The hours I can give the business are capped by being home for my family — so growth has to come from better systems, not more hours.**

The bottleneck is not leads or skill; it is the limited, fixed number of focused work hours available each week. Every initiative is judged by whether it produces more business per hour or buys hours back — not by whether it is "more work."
