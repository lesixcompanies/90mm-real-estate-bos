# Future Reality Tree (FRT)

## Purpose
If the EC says "challenge this assumption and do this instead," the
FRT asks: "Okay, if we do that, what happens? Does the future actually
look better? Does the constraint resolve? Do the symptoms improve?"

## Process
1. Start with the injection from the EC
2. Trace forward: "If [injection], then what happens?"
3. Continue the chain: "And if that happens, then what?"
4. Check whether the chain leads to the desired outcomes:
   - Does the constraint resolve?
   - Do the original symptoms improve?
   - Does throughput (closings, revenue, pipeline) increase?
5. Identify where the chain might break or produce unwanted effects
   → those become inputs for Negative Branch Reservations
6. Present the future state to the agent and ask:
   "Does this future make sense to you? Is this the direction you
   want to go? What concerns do you have?"

## How to Present It
Walk through it conversationally. Show the cause-and-effect chain
from the injection to the outcomes. Be specific — use real numbers,
real timelines, and real scenarios from the agent's own diagnostic.

"If you design and ship the postcard by May 15, here's what happens.
150 households see your name and a personalized message. Even at a
conservative 1-2% response rate, that's 1-3 conversations over the
summer. Given that homes in your neighborhood sit for months with
no marketing, you're entering a market with zero competition for
attention..."

## Resistance Layer Addressed
- **Layer 4: "We disagree on the details of the solution."**
  Ask: "Do you agree with these specifics? Is there anything here
  that doesn't match how you see it playing out?"
  If disagreement → Adjust the FRT. The agent knows their market
  better than the system does. Modify details until the future
  state is one the agent recognizes as realistic.
  Layer 4 must be resolved before moving to NBR.

## Output
- The future state described in concrete terms
- The cause-and-effect chain from injection to outcomes
- Any points where the chain might break (flagged for NBR)
- Agent's agreement with the details (Layer 4 resolved)
- Save to diagnostics/ as part of the change sequence record

## What Happens Next
Run Negative Branch Reservations on any concerns or potential problems
identified during FRT construction. Then, once validated, move to
Strategy & Tactics Tree for execution planning.
